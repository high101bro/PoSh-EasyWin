Adversaries can create new accounts to provide another means of access to a machine
Check for the following when analyzing accounts and groups:
--- Prior staff or service accounts being used
--- Odd logon times and locations 
--- Incorrect account names
--- Accounts having improper privileges
--- Simultaneous logins for one user
--- Account/User lockouts or repeated unsuccessful logins
--- Root/Admin access to critical systems
--- Brute force logins
